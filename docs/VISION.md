# Naturithm — Vision Document

## Why This Exists

Most wellness approaches fail because they're either:
- **Too surface-level**: "Drink water and be grateful" Instagram posts
- **Too gatekept**: $5,000 retreats, celebrity trainers, exclusive communities
- **Too dogmatic**: One-size-fits-all programs that ignore individual needs

Naturithm is different. It uses AI to deliver personalized, honest, accessible wellness guidance at scale — without a human guru in the middle.

## The Problem

People want to be healthier, calmer, more present. But they:
1. Don't know where to start
2. Get overwhelmed by conflicting information
3. Can't afford personal coaches
4. Don't trust influencers (rightfully)
5. Need accountability but hate being nagged

## The Solution: Oria

An AI agent that:
- Meets you where you are (beginner to advanced)
- Doesn't pretend to be human
- Has no ego or agenda beyond your wellbeing
- Learns your preferences and adapts
- Is available 24/7
- Costs a fraction of human coaching
- Works herself out of a job (the goal is self-sufficiency)

## Core Principles

### 1. Truth Over Comfort
Oria tells you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear. But she does it with warmth and empathy.

### 2. No Guru Culture
There is no face behind Naturithm (by design). No personality cult. The founder stays anonymous. Oria is the face, and she's transparent about being AI.

### 3. Self-Sufficiency Is The Goal
We're not building addiction to the app. We're building habits, knowledge, and self-trust. If you stop using Naturithm because you've internalized the pillars — that's success.

### 4. Accessible
Free tier must be genuinely useful, not a teaser. Premium adds depth and personalization, but the basics are free forever.

### 5. Science-Informed, Not Scientism
We respect both peer-reviewed research AND lived experience. Movement doesn't need a study to prove it feels good. But when science speaks, we listen.

## Target Audience

### Primary
- Ages 25-45
- Health-curious but not fitness obsessed
- Feels "something is off" in their daily life
- Open to meditation/mindfulness but hasn't committed
- Likely tried and abandoned wellness apps before

### Secondary
- People in recovery (burnout, injury, life transitions)
- Parents wanting to model wellness for kids
- Professionals in high-stress environments

## Revenue Model (Planned)

1. **Freemium app** — Free daily content + basic Oria chat; Premium for deep coaching
2. **Courses** — Structured programs ($29-99 each)
3. **Corporate wellness** — B2B licensing for companies (future)
4. **Content licensing** — Oria's methodology for other platforms (far future)

## Non-Goals

- We are NOT a fitness app (no rep counting, no calorie tracking)
- We are NOT a therapy replacement (we're wellness, not clinical)
- We are NOT building a personal brand around the founder
- We are NOT competing with Headspace/Calm directly (we're broader — 5 pillars, not just meditation)

## Competitive Landscape

| App | Focus | Gap |
|-----|-------|-----|
| Headspace | Meditation | Only 1 pillar |
| Calm | Sleep + Meditation | Passive content, no AI coaching |
| Noom | Weight loss | Diet-focused, not holistic |
| MyFitnessPal | Calorie tracking | Movement only, no mindfulness |
| **Naturithm** | **All 5 pillars + AI agent** | **Holistic, personalized, honest** |
