## Course Outlines (Premium)

### "30 Days of Stillness"
Week 1: Just showing up (2 min/day)
Week 2: Exploring techniques (breathwork, body scan, mantra)
Week 3: Building the habit (finding your time, place, style)
Week 4: Integration (stillness in daily life, not just on the cushion)

### "Movement Discovery" 
Week 1: Movement audit (what do you do? what have you tried?)
Week 2: Exploration (try 5 different movement forms)
Week 3: Joy check (which ones made you feel alive?)
Week 4: Build your practice (consistency plan, no gym required)

### "Digital Presence Reset"
Week 1: Awareness (track your screen time without judgment)
Week 2: Identify triggers (when/why do you reach for your phone?)
Week 3: Replace habits (what could you do instead?)
Week 4: New normal (intentional tech use as a practice)
