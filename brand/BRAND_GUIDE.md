## Reel Production Pipeline
1. **Video clips**: Use FREE stock video (Pexels/Pixabay) for generic shots (food prep, nature, spices). Only use Veo for unique Oria character shots.
2. Generate voice with TTS (Studio O) — nearly free
3. Compile with moviepy (Palatino overlays: white regular + sand bold italic for key line)
4. Mix audio with ffmpeg sidechain compression (music ducks under voice)
5. Music source: Mixkit (royalty-free, no attribution)
6. Watermark: "oria" top-left on all clips except closing
