# Naturithm

**AI-powered wellness, for everyone.**

Naturithm uses AI to bring wellness to people at scale — no guru, no influencer, just an honest AI agent named **Oria** who helps humans open their eyes and take the first step.

## The Vision

Most wellness content is either too shallow (Instagram quotes) or too gatekept (expensive retreats, celebrity coaches). Naturithm sits in between: accessible, honest, and deeply personal — powered by AI.

The goal? Get people to the point where they **don't need us anymore**.

## The 5 Pillars

Everything in Naturithm is built on 5 pillars:

### 1. 🏃 Movement
Energy generator — not a specific sport or exercise regime. Movement is about finding what makes YOUR body feel alive. Could be running, yoga, dancing, climbing, swimming. The point is: move.

### 2. 🧘 Stillness
Meditation and mindfulness. Learning to be quiet, to observe your own mind. Not religious, not mystical — practical tools for mental clarity and emotional regulation.

### 3. 🥗 Nourishment
Natural nutrition. Eating real food, understanding what fuels your body. Not a diet plan — a relationship with food. Whole foods, minimal processing, listening to your body.

### 4. 🌿 Presence
Be here now. Presence is about attention — where you put it, how you manage it. In a world designed to distract, presence is a superpower.

### 5. 🤝 Trust
Trust yourself. Your body knows more than you think. Your intuition is a signal, not noise. Trust is about rebuilding the connection between mind and body that modern life erodes.

## App Concept

### Free Tier
- Daily wellness content (one pillar per day, rotating)
- Habit tracking (simple, not gamified to death)
- Community wisdom / shared experiences
- Oria's daily insight

### Premium Tier
- Personalized Oria conversations (AI coaching)
- Structured courses (e.g., "30 Days of Stillness", "Movement Foundations")
- Deep-dive content on each pillar
- Progress tracking and personalized recommendations
